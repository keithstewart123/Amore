We are Amore - Est. 1968

How does a small upstairs shop in Naples rise to dominate the world of fashion? We'll let you know when we're finished! In the meantime, our products can be found at the finest retailers in over twenty-five countries and have won multiple accolades from around the globe.

We began and continue with one simple goal. To use the best techniques and technologies available to help you protect your eyes and look incredible doing it.

Our designers and artisans are leaders in the industry and strive to bring the finest quality and most exciting products to market.

We may even be too hip. Too edgy. Perhaps we're not for everyone. But we're not everyone...we are Amore.
